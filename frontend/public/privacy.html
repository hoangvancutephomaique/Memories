Privacy Policy

Memories of Vân — Last updated May 2026

What this app is

Memories of Vân is a personal guestbook where visitors can leave a short message. Facebook Login is used solely to confirm that a real person is signing, not to collect or share your Facebook data.

Information we collect

  • Your public name from Facebook — retrieved via the Facebook Graph API when you sign in. It is stored privately in the database and is only visible to the site owner. It is never shown publicly.
  • The display name you type (optional) — shown publicly on your guestbook entry. If you leave it blank, "Anonymous" is used.
  • Your message — shown publicly on the guestbook.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your Facebook password or email address
  • Your Facebook friends list, timeline, photos, or any other Facebook data
  • Any tracking cookies or analytics data

How your data is used

Your Facebook name is used only to verify identity and is stored privately. Your public display name and message are displayed on the guestbook page. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data retention and deletion

Your guestbook entry (public display name + message) remains until the site owner removes it. Your private Facebook name is stored alongside the entry and is deleted when the entry is deleted.

To request deletion of your data, please open an issue or contact the site owner via the GitHub repository: github.com/hoangvancutephomaique/Memories .

Facebook Login

This app uses Facebook Login provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. By signing in you also agree to Facebook's Data Policy . This app only requests the public_profile permission.

Changes to this policy

This page will be updated if the data practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
